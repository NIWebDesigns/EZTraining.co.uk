About Us

We are a local company based in Newtownabbey, Northern Ireland, taking pride in our work in order to deliver the key knowledge & skills to help people get through their course successfully, as well as gain the necessary qualities to work within the Private Security Industry and hopefully excel.

The Trainer has over 20 years experience working within the Private Security Industry, with most of this time being in various Security Management and training roles. Best practice is shared during the courses, helping you to provide a good service to your company and to the general public. Security is helping to provide a safe and secure environment, and helping customers have an enjoyable experience.
